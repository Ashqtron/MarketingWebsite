The App Features

Here are the awesome things that our App can do

  • Register

    New users can register to the App

  • Login

    Returning users can login quickly

  • Home

    From the Home screen, Users can acces several Options

  • Rating system

    Userc can rate Branches to aid other users on satisfactory customer service

  • Visit a Branch

    Choose a closeby Bank to visit from the Map

  • Select a Branch Service

    Select a Service from the listed active services

  • View and Join Queue

    View real time bank Queue and request to Join Bank Queue

  • Get Queue Token and Go to Branch

    Save your Queue Token and travel to Branch within specified time

About iQueue

Our team’s mission is to help banks provide customers with satisfactory branch visits experience by keeping the bank’s branches and services a few clicks away from their clients, by the integration of manual systems and online system, Bit Rebels aim to provide a real-time experience to the users which allows them to gain more insight and access to their banks without triggering emotions of frustration about their visit at the bank. We are fascinated about the idea of having a bank in our hands.
